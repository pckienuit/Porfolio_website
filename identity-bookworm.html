Bookworm
My Little Angel - Nguyen Nhat Anh (2008)

This book was the first to introduce me to tales of tender and beautiful love, allowing me to perceive the beauty of life's realities not through my eyes, but through the lens of my soul. And it is the one that has always reminded me of Mother Teresa's words:

“Spread love everywhere you go. Let no one ever come without leaving happier.”

Psychological Profile: Psychiatrist or Madman - Muc Qua (2018)

The second factor that ignited my passion for psychology is this book. While it might seem to contain only stories and theoretical lessons, it was far more than that. Instead, it serves as a gateway to a world of profound narratives, allowing me to witness the beauty inherent in wounded souls. After reading this book, I realized that pursuing a career in psychology was no longer a fleeting whim but rather a decision stemming from both my heart and mind.
